Our Story, Values & Mission

Discover what makes WeBuild the premier construction company in Botswana

Our Locations

Headquartered in Gaborone's bustling Ext 12, our main office serves as the hub for all our operations. This 5,000 square meter facility houses our administrative staff, design team, and project management offices.

We have strategically located regional offices in:

  • Francistown: Serving the northern regions with a focus on industrial projects
  • Maun: Specializing in eco-tourism and sustainable building projects
  • Serowe: Handling residential developments in the central district

Our network of satellite offices allows us to maintain close relationships with clients and communities throughout Botswana while ensuring local economic benefits from our projects.

Our Philosophy

At WeBuild, we believe construction is more than just putting up buildings - it's about creating spaces that inspire, protect, and serve communities for generations to come.

Our core philosophical principles:

Community-Centric Approach

Every project must benefit not just the client but the surrounding community through job creation, skills development, and thoughtful design.

Sustainable Future

We prioritize environmentally responsible practices that minimize ecological impact while maximizing energy efficiency.

Ethical Foundations

Integrity guides every decision, from material sourcing to subcontractor relationships and client communications.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

"To deliver exceptional construction services through innovative solutions, uncompromising quality, and steadfast commitment to our clients' visions, while fostering sustainable development and creating value for all stakeholders."

Vision Statement

"To be Botswana's most trusted construction partner, recognized for transforming landscapes and communities through building excellence, and setting the standard for sustainable development in Southern Africa."

Strategic Goals

  • Maintain 98% client satisfaction across all projects
  • Achieve zero workplace accidents through enhanced safety programs
  • Reduce carbon footprint by 25% through green building initiatives
  • Train 500 local workers in specialized construction skills by 2025
  • Expand service offerings to include smart building technologies
